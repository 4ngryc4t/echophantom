Community engagement

Our mission is to promote active participation from the Aleph Zero community to enhanche its interaction with the ecosystem.
Echo, born to engage the community and foster space familiarity, remains a driving force in incentivizing exploration, utilization, and sharing, while our organized events aim to spark discovery, facilitate sharing, and attract new interest, ultimately expanding Aleph Zero's reach.

Flexible Yield-Generation

Moreover, a crucial aspect of this project lies in its role as a flexible yield-generating NFT collection.
Each NFT acts as an instrument to create income: individuals can purchase an NFT, ensuring them future passive earnings by staking the NFT in various pools with different tokens.

Launchpad

By leveraging its presence and network within the community, Echo will provide a platform for promising projects to debut and thrive.
Projects will be vetted for their innovation, utility, and alignment with the values of Aleph Zero. Once onboarded, Echo will offer comprehensive support, including marketing and various promotional events aimed at enhancing the project's visibility.
